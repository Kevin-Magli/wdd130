Overview

Purpose

To provide information from different types of replica Lightsaber makers ranging from cheap toys to collector edition lightsabers. You'll find where and how to buy them and everything you need to know to find the best lightsaber for your choice.

Audience

This website is aimed at people of all ages, in any business situation, Star Wars fans who dream of having their own lightsaber but feel overwhelmed by the process of buying it. This website is here to simplify this task and it'll be available in every platform

Content

Home page

The home page will feature a scrolling layout with banners for the most visited sites to buy lightsabers or to customize your own. There will be more than just 4 options but the first options on the page will be sites or products that have a limited time discount. The header will contain the link for page 2: Advanced Filter and Page 3: About us.
The very first option will contain a sublink to the site partners The subpage will contain featured offers with a lower price.

Images for the Home page

Advanced Find

This page will contain a search bar to find the products you are looking for. Also there will be a filter that will let you choose the type of products you are looking for by very specific categories. The majority of those options are multi-selectable to allow you to have even more specific results as the aim of the site is to provide the most options available in the internet. This page will lead you to a sub-page with products that match the search. All the filters will be at the left of the page and the results will appear as scrolling containers with information about the products. Every product will have tags and provider information, clickling on them will lead you to the product official page.
There will be a mechanism that gathers products that match the specifications provided by me. It will search many sites on the internet and retrieve the product data such as price, location(if available) an image of the product and many other information. Each product added will countain a series of tags that are directly connected to the filters. The search bar will also be directly connected to the filters identifying the keywords used by the user.
